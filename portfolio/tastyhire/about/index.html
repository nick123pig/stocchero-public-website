About Tasty Hire

Tasty Hire was born from the desire to give all people the opportunity to introduce themselves to employers, regardless of their education or prior work experience.

While many companies have attempted to bring a more personal approach to the job search, none have been able to incorporate a first impression at the forefront of the process. With a 30-second video, job searchers can now introduce themselves to thousands of potential employers with exceptional ease. This enables employers to meet every candidate before screening résumés.
